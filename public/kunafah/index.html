Cream Kunafah Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (16 oz) of kataifi dough (shredded phyllo dough)
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the kataifi dough by separating and fluffing the strands with your fingers.
  3. Drizzle the melted butter over the kataifi dough and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Press half of the buttered kataifi dough into the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch baking dish to form a crust.
  5. In a saucepan, combine the heavy cream, milk, sugar, and cornstarch. Heat over medium heat, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the cream mixture over the kataifi crust in the baking dish.
  7. Take the remaining half of the buttered kataifi dough and spread it over the cream layer, pressing gently.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-55 minutes or until the kunafah is golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove the kunafah from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  10. Garnish with chopped pistachios.
  11. Serve your delicious Cream Kunafah warm and enjoy!

Note: Cream Kunafah is best served warm and can be drizzled with extra syrup or dusted with powdered sugar for added sweetness.
